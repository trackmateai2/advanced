Privacy Policy

Welcome To TrackMate
We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our App and services.

1. Information We Collect
1.1. IMEI Data
We collect and store the IMEI number of mobile devices you register with our app. The IMEI is used solely for tracking purposes and is treated as personally identifiable information (PII).
1.2. Personal Information
When using our services, we may collect personal data such as:
- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Device information (model, operating system, etc.)
1.3. Location Data
Depending on the features of the app, we may collect location data from the device using GPS or network-based methods. This data will be used only for the purpose of tracking the registered mobile device.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:
- To track mobile devices via IMEI numbers.
- To improve the functionality and user experience of the app.
- To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues.
- To send notifications regarding updates to the service, new features, or legal obligations.
3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal data or IMEI tracking information with third parties unless it is:
- Necessary to protect the security and integrity of our systems.
- Shared with service providers who perform functions on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.
4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement a variety of measures to safeguard your personal information and IMEI data. These measures include:
- Encryption of IMEI data.
- Secure data transmission using SSL.
- Restricting access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
Despite these precautions, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.
5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as it is necessary to provide you with the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request the deletion of your data at any time, but note that this may affect the functionality of the app.
6. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data and IMEI data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Object to the processing of your data.
- Request data portability.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [trackmateai@gmail.com].
7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take steps to delete that information.
8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our App. Your continued use of the App after the changes take effect signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
